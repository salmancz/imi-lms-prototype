We are sorry to see you go. Before you continue, please review what happens to your account and data.

What happens when you delete

  • Your account is soft-deleted immediately. You can restore it within 30 days.
  • All sessions are terminated and login is disabled.
  • In-progress courses are paused. Progress is preserved during the 30-day grace.
  • After 30 days, all PII (name, email, contact info) is purged permanently.
  • If you are a locally certified learner, your record is never hard deleted and is retained permanently by membership number for verification.
  • Access to your issued Course Completed proofs remains functional even after your account is deactivated, suspended, dormant, or deleted.
  • If you have a corporate account, your tenant admin is notified.
