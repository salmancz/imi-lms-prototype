Most asked
I did not receive my OTP code

Check your spam folder first. OTPs expire after 2 min. If still missing, click Resend on the verification screen. After 3 failed verifications, your account locks for 24 hr.

Why can't I download my Course Completed proof?

Course Completed proof issuance requires 100% profile completion including optional fields. Visit Profile > Edit and complete every section.

How do I change the name on my Course Completed proof?

Edit your Course Completed Name in Profile. OTP verification required. Note: changes apply to FUTURE Course Completed proofs only, already issued ones retain their original name.

My quiz timer ran out, can I resume?

Quiz resume is NOT supported. Exiting mid-quiz means restarting from question 1. The quiz start warning page details this before you begin.

How many devices can I use?

Max 2 registered devices, only 1 active session at a time. Sign out from one device before signing in on a third.
