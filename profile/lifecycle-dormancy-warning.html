90 days until auto-deletion
What happens if you stay inactive
  • Now (Day 90)
    This warning. Your account is still fully active. All data, progress, and certificates are intact.
  • Day 150 (30 days before)
    A final warning email is sent. Account is read-only: you can view and download but not enrol or complete courses.
  • Day 180 (auto-deletion)
    Account is deleted automatically. Profile, enrolled courses, saved notes, and wishlist are permanently removed. A 30-day grace period follows for recovery.
  • Permanently retained
    Your Course Completed proofs are linked to your membership number and remain verifiable indefinitely, even after account deletion.
