What is knowledge mapping

Lesson 1 · Video · 12 min · Free preview
Preview · 12 min

In this introductory lesson, you will explore the foundational concept of knowledge mapping: what it is, why organisations use it, and how it connects to broader knowledge management frameworks.

By the end of this lesson, you will be able to define knowledge mapping, distinguish it from related practices like process mapping and mind mapping, and explain its role within ISO 30401.
